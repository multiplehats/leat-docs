Three checkout block integrations come with the plugin. They slot into the WooCommerce block checkout and give customers a way to interact with their loyalty account without leaving the checkout flow.

## Checkout blocks

**Points display**
Shows customers how many credits they will earn on the current order before they place it.

**Spend rules redemption**
Lets customers redeem an available reward at checkout. The reward converts to a WooCommerce coupon and is applied to the order.

**Gift card input**
A code entry field where customers apply a gift card balance to their order. Supports partial redemption, so the remaining balance stays on the card.
