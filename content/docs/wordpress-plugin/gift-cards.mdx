The plugin handles the full gift card lifecycle: creating them when an order completes, emailing them to the recipient, and letting customers redeem them through the checkout block.

- Enabling gift card acceptance at checkout

- Partial redemption (spending less than the full balance)
- Checking a gift card balance
- Cancelling gift cards on order refund or cancellation
- Customizing all gift card UI text
