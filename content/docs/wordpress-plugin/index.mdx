The Leat plugin connects WooCommerce to your Leat loyalty account. Customers earn credits on purchases, redeem rewards at checkout, and manage everything through a loyalty dashboard you build with Gutenberg blocks.

## What the plugin does

**Loyalty program**

Customers earn credits based on rules you define, for example a fixed amount per order or a percentage of the order total. Credits accumulate and can be redeemed for discounts through spend rules. You control which order statuses trigger earning and which trigger refunds.

**Rewards and spend rules**

Rewards are pulled from your Leat dashboard and made available in WooCommerce as coupons. Each reward converts to a WooCommerce coupon when a customer claims it. You set minimum spend, individual use, expiry, and usage limits.

**Gift cards**

Sell gift cards as WooCommerce products. The plugin creates and sends the gift card to the recipient on order completion. Customers redeem gift cards at checkout through a dedicated input in the checkout block. Partial redemption is supported.

**Vouchers**

Promotion rules generate voucher codes that get emailed to customers. Vouchers work as WooCommerce coupons with configurable discounts, expiry, usage limits, and minimum spend requirements.

**Prepaid products**

Sell products that top up a customer's prepaid balance. When the order completes, the plugin adds the purchased amount to their Leat balance.

**Tiers**

Display your loyalty tier structure to customers, show their current tier and progress, and apply tier-based earning multipliers through your earn rules.

**Referrals**

Each customer gets a referral link. When someone signs up using that link, the referral is tracked and rewarded. The dashboard shows how many referrals a customer has made and how many converted.

**Promotions**

Create and manage promotional campaigns with percentage or fixed discounts. Tie promotions to specific products or categories, set usage caps, and distribute voucher codes automatically.

**Gutenberg blocks and patterns**

A dedicated block category gives you building blocks for every part of the loyalty experience: points display, earn rules list, rewards list, coupons, tiers, referrals, and checkout integrations. Pre-built patterns let you drop a complete loyalty dashboard onto any page in seconds.

**Checkout integrations**

Blocks for the WooCommerce checkout show points being earned on the current order, let customers redeem rewards, and handle gift card code entry, all inside the standard block checkout.

**My Account integration**

A loyalty tab appears in the customer's WooCommerce account page. You control the tab label and the content comes from your configured dashboard page.

**Multi-language**

All customer-facing text is translatable through the plugin settings, with fields for each locale. English, Dutch (NL), and Dutch (BE) ship out of the box.
