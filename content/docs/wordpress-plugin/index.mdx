title: WordPress Plugin
description: Overview of the Leat WooCommerce plugin
---

The Leat plugin connects WooCommerce to your Leat loyalty account. Customers earn credits on purchases, redeem rewards at checkout, and manage everything through a loyalty dashboard you build with Gutenberg blocks.

## What the plugin does

<Cards>
  <Card title="Loyalty program" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/earn-rules">
    Customers earn credits based on rules you define: fixed amounts per order, percentage of the total, or custom triggers. Credits accumulate and can be redeemed for discounts through spend rules.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rewards & spend rules" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/rewards">
    Rewards sync from your Leat dashboard and convert to WooCommerce coupons when claimed. Configure minimum spend, individual use, expiry, and usage limits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gift cards" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/gift-cards">
    Sell gift cards as WooCommerce products. The plugin sends them on order completion. Customers redeem at checkout with partial redemption supported.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vouchers" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/vouchers">
    Promotion rules generate voucher codes emailed to customers. Vouchers work as WooCommerce coupons with configurable discounts, expiry, and usage limits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prepaid products" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/prepaid-products">
    Sell products that top up a customer's prepaid balance. The purchased amount is added to their Leat balance on order completion.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tiers" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/tiers">
    Display your loyalty tier structure, show customers their current tier and progress, and apply tier-based earning multipliers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referrals" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/referrals">
    Each customer gets a referral link. Signups through that link are tracked and rewarded, with conversion stats on the dashboard.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Promotions" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/promotions">
    Run campaigns with percentage or fixed discounts. Tie them to specific products or categories, set usage caps, and distribute codes automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blocks & patterns" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/dashboard">
    A full set of Gutenberg blocks for points, rewards, tiers, referrals, and checkout. Pre-built patterns drop a complete loyalty dashboard onto any page.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Checkout" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/checkout">
    Show points earned on the current order, let customers redeem rewards, and handle gift card entry, all inside the WooCommerce block checkout.
  </Card>
  <Card title="My Account" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/settings">
    A loyalty tab in the customer's WooCommerce account page. You control the tab label and content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-language" href="/docs/wordpress-plugin/settings">
    All customer-facing text is translatable through plugin settings. English, Dutch (NL), and Dutch (BE) ship out of the box.
  </Card>
</Cards>
